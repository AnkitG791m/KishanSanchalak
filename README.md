KishanSanchalak
AI-Powered Offline-First Farmer Support Platform

KishanSanchalak is a technology-driven agriculture support platform designed to solve real problems faced by farmers in rural and low-connectivity areas. The system works even without internet access or smartphones and focuses on farmer safety, fair income, and digital inclusion.

Problem Statement

Farmers face multiple challenges in daily agricultural activities. Most farmers do not have access to high-end smartphones, stable internet, or continuous electricity. Identifying crop diseases correctly is difficult, which often leads to wrong treatment and crop loss. Fake or unsuitable medicines are commonly sold, causing further damage. Natural disasters such as floods, heatwaves, and storms arrive without warning. During crop selling, middlemen exploit farmers by offering unfair prices. In addition, workers are often unavailable during peak farming seasons, and many farmers miss government subsidies due to lack of information.

Solution Overview

KishanSanchalak provides a single platform that supports farmers from crop health monitoring to safe selling and emergency alerts. The system is designed for village environments and works across smartphones and basic keypad phones.

Core Features

Offline crop health detection using image analysis
Medicine verification to avoid fake or harmful products
Early disaster alerts delivered through mobile notifications and SMS
Keypad phone support using SMS-based communication
Family emergency alert system if farmer does not respond
On-demand access to nearby agricultural workers
Verified buyers with government-approved crop prices
One-click reporting for unfair pricing or exploitation
Subsidy awareness and eligibility notifications
Seasonal learning and advisory support

Flow of Solution

Farmer registration using any mobile phone
Family members linked for emergency communication
Farm and crop details recorded
Crop image analyzed offline
Disease identified and solution suggested
Medicine verified before use
Disaster risks monitored continuously
Alerts sent via app and SMS
Emergency escalation to family if no response
Worker availability checked and contacted
Crop selling with fair price visibility
Complaint sent automatically if price is unfair
Subsidy eligibility matched and notified
Farmer receives guidance and support

Feasibility

The solution uses existing mobile devices and does not require new hardware. Offline functionality and SMS support make it practical for rural deployment. The system can be rolled out village by village at low cost and scaled with institutional support.

Challenges

Maintaining accurate local data

Competitors

Weather applications focus only on forecasts and require internet access.
Agriculture advisory apps depend on smartphones and data connectivity.
Market platforms focus only on pricing.
KishanSanchalak combines all these aspects into one offline-capable farmer support system.
